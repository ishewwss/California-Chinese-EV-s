Why Create a Website for This Project?

For this Multi‑Modal Project, I chose to build a website because it presents the information in a way that feels modern, hands on, and easy. Instead of reading a long essay, a website lets people read the ideas in their own pace. Each page focuses on a specific topic, which makes the information clearer and more organized.

My target audience are advocacyy groups and state policy makers. While most policy makers gain their Information from hearings, meetings, and advocy groups websites are mainly used as resources. Im also targeting advocacy groups because they usually present their findings to state board members. Using a website would mean it hits all groups and is used.

I chose State policy makers because they have the power to change laws in a specific state. According to the tenth admenemdment of the U.S consituition the states have the power to choose whats legal or illegal.

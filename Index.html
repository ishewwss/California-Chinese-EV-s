Chinese Electric Vehicles in California

Exploring why Chinese EVs like the BYD U9 could play a big role in California’s future.

By Andrew Velazquez · Multi-Modal Project (MMP)

Why Focus on Chinese Vehicles?

Electric cars are becoming more common in California, but a lot of them are still too expensive for regular people, especially students and families. Chinese electric vehicles offer a different option: they’re usually cheaper, still packed with technology, and could help more people switch away from gas cars faster.

This project looks at three main reasons why Chinese EVs could actually be a good thing for California: they help the environment, they increase competition and lower prices, and they bring in advanced technology that pushes the whole industry forward.
